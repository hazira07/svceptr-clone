About Us

S.V. College of Engineering (SVCE) was founded by ”SAMPATHI EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY” at Nagari-Narayanavanam Road, Ippathangale (V) in 2024. It is located in Puttur, Tirupati Dist.

SVCE is connected to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University in Ananthapur, Andhra Pradesh. The Institute follows the curriculum and plans from the university. We have a schedule for the school year that comes from the university. We plan what we'll do each semester and make a list of events. At the start of each semester, teachers plan what they'll teach. Students have tests during the semester, following the schedule. If some students need extra help, we have special classes to catch up. For laboratory classes, teachers make manuals to help students in labs. We also take students on trips to see real-world examples of what they're learning. These trips help connect what they learn in class to how it's used in real life.
